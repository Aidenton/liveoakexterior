About Live Oak Exterior

Started from nothing. Built on this coast.

Meet the Owner

I started cleaning windows as a side hustle in 2025. I was 16, a junior in high school. My dad told me if I cleaned the windows around our house, he'd pay me for it. That's where it started.

In 2026 I launched Live Oak Exterior as a real brand. The name comes from the live oak trees all over coastal NC. I've always loved those trees. They're tough, deep-rooted, and they belong here. That's what I want this business to be.

I grew up in Surf City and moved to Hampstead with my family. There are five of us, and this coast is home.

Right now we are running a small team to insure quality work. Every job we take on is done by someone on my crew. No subcontractors. If a window is still streaky when we leave, we guarantee to come back and fix it for free.

My goal is to build an easy access, affordable, and customer friendly way to get exterior cleaning done on this coast. I run this business on faith in God, hard work, and discipline. Those aren't just words I say. They're how every job gets done.

Aiden Benton, Owner, Live Oak Exterior
