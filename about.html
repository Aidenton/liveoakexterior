Who We Are

Aiden started Live Oak Exterior in high school with a squeegee and a bucket. That was it.

He didn't want to spend his early twenties working for someone else. So he picked something he could start cheap, learn fast, and actually be good at. Window cleaning checked all three boxes.

The first jobs came from knocking on doors in coastal neighborhoods around Hampstead and Wilmington. Then those customers told their neighbors. Then their neighbors told theirs. That's still how a lot of new work comes in.

The name comes from the trees lining the roads around here. Tough, deep-rooted, built for salt air and storms. That's the kind of business this is meant to be. No flash. Just solid work that holds up.

Right now it's Aiden and one other guy he's training. Every job is done by one of them. No subcontractors. No shortcuts. If a window is still streaky when they leave, they're coming back to fix it. His name is on the truck.

More services are on the way. Soft washing, driveways, gutters. But the way the work gets done doesn't change. Show up when you said you would. Do it right the first time. Leave the place looking better than you found it.

That's the whole thing.

Aiden Benton — Owner & Operator, Live Oak Exterior
