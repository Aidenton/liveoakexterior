Services

Exterior cleaning for homes and businesses across coastal NC.

Window Cleaning

Salt air, pollen, and humidity hit coastal windows hard — we live here, so we know what that looks like. We clean interior and exterior glass, frames, sills, tracks, and screens for homes and businesses alike. For commercial work, we schedule around your hours: early morning, late evening, whatever keeps your operation running. Before we leave, we do a final walkthrough. If something isn't right, we come back and fix it.

Driveway Pressure Washing

Driveways, sidewalks, and patios. Oil stains, mildew, algae — surface cleaner for an even, streak-free finish. Good for pre-sale prep or knocking out years of coastal grime.

Gutter Cleaning

Clogged gutters cause real damage. We clear the debris, flush the downspouts, and verify drainage is working the way it should. Best done before storm season or after the leaves come down.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Salt air, pollen, and dust build up quietly on coastal panels and cut your output. We clean them with the right tools so your system runs the way it's supposed to. Ask about recurring maintenance plans.

Streak-free guarantee on all window work. If anything isn't right when we leave, we come back and fix it — no charge.
